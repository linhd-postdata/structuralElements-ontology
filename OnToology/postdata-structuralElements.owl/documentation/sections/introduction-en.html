Introduction back to ToC

The postdata-structuralElements ontology covers the aspects relating to the formal structure of a poem.

We defne the classes: Line, Stanza , Syllable, Word and Punctuation.
These last two classes are part of a hierarchy based on the Token class.

Postdata-core ontology has been imported to model the relationship of the structures with the redaction of which they are part.

On the other hand, properties have also been defined that relate the Line with Agent and Place, to treat the mentions to people, organizations and places that appear in the lines.

Prosodic features and elements such as metric syllables, feet and metric patterns are treated in the postdata-prosodicElements ontology, which imports this ontology.
